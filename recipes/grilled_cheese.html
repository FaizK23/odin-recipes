The Deluxe Grilled Cheese

Why this recipe is a must make!

I'm not a big sandwhich person myself, but when I tried this grilled cheese for the first time, I knew it would not be the last time I made it


Ingredients:

  • 2 slices sourdough bread
  • 1 slice gruyere
  • 1 slice Parmesan
  • 1 slice yellow cheddar
  • 1 slice white cheddar
  • Softened unsalted butter, for brushing
  • Olive oil

How to make this Life Changing Sandwhich

  1. Baste bread slices on one side with butter.
  2. Drizzle olive oil on medium-hot griddle.
  3. Put buttered bread slices on griddle (butter side down).
  4. Add cheeses on one bread slice and move bread around on griddle as cheese melts.
  5. Check color of bread slices - when golden brown, join bread.
  6. Butter top of sandwich lightly again, flip, butter new top of sandwich. Keep moving sandwich around for 5 min.

  8. Lightly baste bread with more butter and enjoy!
