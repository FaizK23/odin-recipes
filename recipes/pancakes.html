Yummy Pacakes

Why this recipe is a must make

Pancakes or Waffles, not trying to get political here but I think this pancake recipe might just convert some of the best waffle lovers out there, like it did for me when I first had them

What you will need:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt, or more to taste
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 3 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 1 large egg

How to make these fluffly pancakes

  1. Mix flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt into a bowl
  2. Mix eggs, milk, and melted butter in second bowl
  3. Mix wet ingredients with dry ingredients, being careful not to overmix
  4. Let batter sit for 10 min
  5. While waiting preheat skillet on medium low heat
  6. Scoop batter with 1/4 cup measure into pan
  7. Flip when bubble form on pancake, and after 2 minutes remove from heat
  8. Repeat until you have enough
  9. Enjoy!
